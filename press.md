      <p style="margin-top: 0;">
        My research on children's privacy, EdTech, online tracking, cybersecurity,
        and AI governance has been featured in national and education-focused media outlets.
        This coverage has focused primarily on how schools and technology platforms collect,
        use, and secure student data, and what accountability should look like when those
        systems fail.
